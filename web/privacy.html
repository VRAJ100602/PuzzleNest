Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This policy explains what data PuzzleNest collects, why, and the rights you have over it — including rights under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

1. Data We Collect

DataWhenWhy
Username & hashed passwordAccount registrationSign-in and identifying your scores
Email address (optional)If you add itPassword recovery and payment receipts
Game stats (wins, times, scores, streaks)While playing signed inLeaderboards, achievements, your stats page
Subscription & transaction recordsIf you buy PremiumManaging your subscription; legal/accounting requirements
Basic server logs (IP, path, timestamp)Every requestSecurity, rate limiting, abuse prevention

We never see your payment details. Cards and UPI are handled entirely by Razorpay; we receive only confirmation identifiers (payment ID, subscription ID).

2. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.
  • We do not run third-party advertising trackers.
  • We do not collect data from players who play without an account, beyond standard server logs and on-device localStorage that never leaves your browser.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to keep you signed in (auth token), remember preferences (difficulty, music), and store offline game stats. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

4. Sharing

Your username, scores, and Premium badge appear on public leaderboards. Data is otherwise shared only with: Razorpay (payment processing) and our hosting provider (server infrastructure), each bound by their own privacy obligations.

5. Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. Transaction records are retained as required by Indian tax and accounting law (typically 8 years). Server logs rotate within 90 days.

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act)

  • Access — ask us what personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — fix inaccurate data (e.g., change your email in Settings).
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data. Transaction records we are legally required to keep are retained in anonymised form where possible.
  • Grievance redressal — contact us (below); we aim to respond within 7 days.

To exercise any right, use the Contact page from the email linked to your account (or include your username).

7. Children

PuzzleNest is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18 beyond what is needed to operate the Service; accounts do not require any real-name information.

8. Security

Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing. Payments use Razorpay's PCI-DSS-compliant infrastructure with signature verification on every webhook. Access to production data is limited to the operator.

9. Changes

We will post any material changes to this policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: see the Contact page.
