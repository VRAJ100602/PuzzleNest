Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Short version: cancel anytime from Settings and you keep Premium until the end of the period you paid for. If something went wrong with a charge — double billing, payment without activation, or a genuine mistake within 48 hours — write to us and we'll refund it.

1. Cancelling Your Subscription

  • Cancel anytime from Settings → Premium Subscription → Cancel. No questions, no retention flows.
  • Cancellation stops future renewals. Your Premium features remain active until the end of the current paid month.
  • After expiry, your account, scores, achievements, and coins are untouched — only Premium features (unlimited hints, archive, badge) switch off.

2. When We Refund

We issue a full refund of the most recent charge in these cases:

  1. Duplicate charge — you were billed more than once for the same period.
  2. Paid but not activated — payment succeeded but Premium did not activate on your account within 24 hours, and our support couldn't fix it.
  3. Accidental purchase — you contact us within 48 hours of the charge and have not meaningfully used Premium features in that time.
  4. Renewal after cancellation — you cancelled before the renewal date but were still charged.

3. When We Don't Refund

  • Partial months — cancelling mid-month doesn't pro-rate the current period (you keep access instead).
  • Charges older than 30 days, except where required by law.
  • Accounts suspended for cheating or abuse of the Service.

4. How to Request a Refund

  1. Go to the Contact page and email us with the subject "Refund request".
  2. Include your PuzzleNest username and (if available) the Razorpay payment ID from your payment confirmation.
  3. We respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed through Razorpay to your original payment method and typically arrive in 5–7 business days, depending on your bank.

5. Failed Renewals

If a renewal payment fails, we do not retry indefinitely — your Premium simply ends at the close of the period already paid for. You can re-subscribe anytime from the pricing page.

6. Questions

Anything unclear, ask first — see the Contact page. We'd rather sort it out than have you dispute a charge.
